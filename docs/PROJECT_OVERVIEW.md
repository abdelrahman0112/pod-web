# People Of Data - Project Overview

## Project Description
A comprehensive community platform for data and AI professionals in Egypt and MENA region. The platform facilitates networking, career opportunities, knowledge sharing, and community engagement through various features.

## Core Mission
Connect data science and AI professionals, provide career opportunities, and foster community growth through events, hackathons, job postings, and educational content.

## Target Audience
- Data Scientists, AI Engineers, ML Engineers
- Data Analysts, Business Intelligence professionals
- Students and fresh graduates in data/AI fields
- Business owners and companies seeking data talent
- Tech recruiters and HR professionals

## Key Stakeholders
- **Community Members**: Primary users engaging with platform features
- **Business Clients**: Companies posting jobs and organizing events
- **Administrators**: Platform moderators and content managers
- **Super Administrators**: Platform owners with full system control

## Platform Objectives
1. **Career Development**: Job opportunities, internships, skill sharing
2. **Community Building**: Events, networking, knowledge exchange
3. **Innovation**: Hackathons, project collaborations
4. **Knowledge Sharing**: Posts, discussions, mentorship
5. **Business Connections**: Client-freelancer matching

## Success Metrics
- User engagement and retention
- Job placement success rate
- Event attendance and satisfaction
- Community content quality
- Platform user growth

## Competitive Advantages
- MENA-focused data community
- Integrated job marketplace
- Hackathon management system
- AI-powered assistance
- Mobile-first experience
